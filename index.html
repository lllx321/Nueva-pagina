About Veloura

Veloura is a sweet little world where homemade flavors meet creative charm. Artisanal desserts, delightful drinks, and crochet-inspired details come together to create memorable experiences.

Desserts are not just meant to be eaten, but admired, shared, and remembered. Each creation is crafted with care and warmth.

Created by Sarah Álvarez, Regina Castañeda, and Marcela Rodríguez, Veloura turns simple moments into something special.
